Overview

The Algorithmic Trading Bootcamp and Hackathon is an 8-week program held from June to July 2025, providing participants with an immersive experience in algorithmic trading. The bootcamp will take place both on-site and online during the first 3 weeks. Hackathon finalists will have a special on-site session and final presentation. Weekly sessions are scheduled for Sundays.

Prerequisite

  • Basic programming concepts (Python).
  • Data processing fundamentals: Handling, cleaning, and normalizing market data.

Bootcamp: Weeks 1-3

Week 1: Introduction to Algorithmic Trading, 1 June 2025

  • Overview of Quant Finance.
  • Differences between algorithmic and traditional trading.
  • Regulations and trends in Thailand.
  • Comparing Crypto Markets to Traditional Markets.

Week 2: Strategy Development and Signal Construction, 8 June 2025

  • Workflow processes in algorithmic trading.
  • Signal construction: Slow/fast cycles, volatility targeting, and filtering noise.
  • Common strategies and their pitfalls.
  • Fundamentals of risk management: Position sizing, drawdown control, and portfolio-level risk controls.

Week 3: Execution Strategies and Backtesting, 15 June 2025

  • Understanding factor models and their applications.
  • Pair trading: Concepts, implementation, and use cases.
  • Momentum strategies: Identifying trends and designing momentum-based trades.
  • Introduction to backtesting frameworks.
  • Hands-on backtesting strategies.

Hackathon: Weeks 4-8

Weeks 4-5: Proposal Submission, 16-29 June 2025

  • No classes.
  • Participants to submit idea proposals without implementation.
  • Announcement of Finalists: 6 July 2025

Week 6: On-Site Engagement, 13 July 2025

  • On-site meeting for all finalist teams.
  • Panel Discussion: Real-World Experiences in Algorithmic Trading.

Weeks 7-8: Final Round of Hackathon, 14-27 July 2025

  • Project development.
  • Weekly check-ins with mentors for feedback.
  • Final Presentation: 27 July 2025
